Privacy Policy

Since May 25, 2018, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) has been in effect in the European Union, establishing uniform requirements for the protection, processing, and circulation of personal data of individuals.

Who We Are

LLC “Panda Store” has developed this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") to demonstrate our commitment to protecting your personal data and to inform you about how we process your personal information.

This Privacy Policy describes our information processing practices regarding the website www.pandastore.store, which we provide to users (hereinafter collectively referred to as "Services"). We will not disclose your personal information to third parties, except as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

How to Contact Us

If you have any questions about how we collect, store, and use your personal information and data, please write to us at [email protected].

Types of Information We Collect and How We Use It

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, as well as data about website visits, as described below.

Personally identifiable information includes: your first and last name, email address, phone number, age, Telegram username, as well as a persistent identifier (e.g., customer number contained in a cookie) associated with personally identifiable information, and other information you may voluntarily provide to us. We collect the following personal information provided by you:

  • to respond to your inquiries;
  • to provide the Service you requested, including processing online purchases and delivering purchased goods to you;
  • to comply with legal requirements;
  • to protect our legitimate interests, including preventing illegal activities;
  • to analyze and improve our operations;
  • to resolve disputes and claims;
  • to tailor and create appropriate product offers for you;
  • to evaluate our products;
  • to organize and conduct contests and promotions;

Website Visit Data

Website visit data is non-personal information about website usage. We use anonymous online activity data to better understand how people use our services. To this end, we may use aggregated anonymous information about traffic, online activities, and third-party information to monitor usage trends and improve our services. We may collect information about:

  • the date and time of access to the website www.pandastore.store;
  • your Internet Protocol (IP) address;
  • products you viewed on our website www.pandastore.store;
  • services you purchased or viewed.

Most website visit data is collected using data collection tools such as web beacons, cookies, embedded web links, and other software (collectively referred to as "Data Collection Tools"). Data Collection Tools are typically small data files or software sent to your browser and stored on your computer’s or mobile device’s hard drive. Most browsers and viewing programs can be configured to notify you of the use of Data Collection Tools or the sending of other tracking software to your computer or personal communication device. They also provide you with the option to refuse the use of such Data Collection Tools. However, refusing to use Data Collection Tools may sometimes prevent you from accessing certain areas or features of the Services or negatively affect their display or functionality.

Data Collection Tools may store information in your shopping cart and allow us to associate your use of the Services with other information about you, including your personal data. We may do this to enhance and personalize your experience with the Services and help resolve issues you may encounter while using the Services.

Legal Grounds for Information Processing

Your personal data is processed based on the following legal grounds:

  • General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR"), Article 6(1)(a) (your consent);
  • Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract);
  • Article 6(1)(c) GDPR (compliance with a legal obligation);
  • Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests of us or third parties);

Our legitimate interests include our business activities, including improving the Services, protecting our legal interests, engaging in commercial transactions, and facilitating the sale of services for "Panda Store".

Information Sharing

"Panda Store" will not disclose any personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following cases. We may disclose collected information, including personally identifiable information, to third parties as required by law. For example, we may disclose information to regulatory authorities and law enforcement upon their official request. "Panda Store" is not responsible for protecting information you provide on other websites. You should be aware that when you voluntarily disclose personal information to other applications and websites, it may be collected and used by others, which could result in receiving unsolicited messages.

Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. In the case of administrative or legal proceedings, we will retain your personal data until the conclusion of such proceedings. If the law requires us to retain your personal data, we will comply with the requirements of the applicable law.

Data Security

Whenever you access an area of the website containing confidential information or where you need to enter such information, a lock icon appears in your browser’s address bar, indicating that the page is secure. www.pandastore.store uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) and private communication technology security standards supported by Microsoft Internet Explorer 4.0 or later, as well as other popular browsers. SSL encrypts your personal information, including your credit card number, name, address, phone number, personal identification number, and password, making it unreadable by unauthorized parties.

While we provide encryption technology and implement other reasonable security measures to protect confidential information and ensure adequate security, we do not guarantee that information transmitted over the Internet is secure or that such transmission will be free from delays, interruptions, interception, or errors.

We have established certain physical, technological, and administrative procedures to protect the personal information we collect. However, these procedures do not guarantee that information will be protected from theft or misuse.

Your Rights

If you wish to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, object to its processing, exercise your right to data portability, or have any questions about the processing of your personal data, please contact us: email us at [email protected].

Data Deletion

As noted above, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data stored with us. If you wish to delete this personal data, please contact us: email us at [email protected]. Upon receiving your request for data deletion, we will act in accordance with the provisions of GDPR.

We will verify whether any of the listed exceptions apply in your case. If personal data is to be deleted upon your request in accordance with GDPR provisions, it will be deleted.

Please note that personal data will not be deleted if its processing is necessary for compliance with legal obligations required by law, as well as if the personal data is necessary to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of November 2024. We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time. If we do so, we will post the current Privacy Policy on this page. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will become effective as of the date of publication.
